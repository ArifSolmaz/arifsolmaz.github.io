Exoplanets around messy stars

Many of my targets do not have quiet, textbook light curves. Rotating starspots, faculae and other activity features distort transit depths, timings and transmission spectra.

I ask questions like: ‚ÄúIf the star really looked like this, what would we actually measure for the planet?‚Äù

Project philosophy

Good student projects are focused and finite. I value:

  • One clear question per project
  • Readable code and labeled figures
  • Transparent limitations and uncertainties
  • Artifacts your future self will understand
