02 — WHY IT MATTERS

The Search for Other Earths

Finding planets beyond our solar system helps us understand how common worlds like Earth might be. By studying these distant planets and their host stars, we learn about conditions that could support life elsewhere—contributing to one of humanity's oldest questions: are we alone?
